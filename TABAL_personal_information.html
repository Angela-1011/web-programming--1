About Me

Hello! My name is Angela Tabal. I am 18 years old. I enjoy a variety of hobbies and have some favorite things that define who I am. Here’s a quick overview of a few things I love:

One of my biggest passions is watching Kdramas. I love how the stories unfold, the emotional depth of the characters, and the unique blend of drama, romance, and comedy. It’s more than just entertainment for me; it’s a way to relax and connect with stories from a different culture.

My favorite color is pink, which reflects my joyful and optimistic personality. Pink is a color that makes me feel warm and happy, and I tend to choose it in my everyday life, from my clothing to the way I style my room.

Whenever I get a chance, I love being surrounded by nature. There’s something incredibly peaceful about being outdoors, whether it’s a walk in the park or spending time in the mountains. Nature recharges me, helping me clear my mind and find balance in my busy life.

I’m always excited to explore new interests and grow in different ways, so this is just a glimpse into the things that make me who I am today. I look forward to discovering even more things I’m passionate about in the future!
